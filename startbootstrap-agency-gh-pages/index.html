About

Black Ridge is an operations and supply chain consulting company specializing in the medical device, drug and food industries. Areas of expertise and focus include operations strategy and execution, supply chain development and optimization, ERP and advanced planning systems, strategic sourcing, process engineering, distribution / logistics and facility network planning. Black Ridge leverages over 30 years of experience with high-growth medical device and technology companies including Johnson & Johnson, Cardinal Healthcare, Allegiance Healthcare, Baxter Healthcare and American Hospital Supply Corporation.
